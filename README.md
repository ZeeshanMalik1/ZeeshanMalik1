### 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm **Zeeshan**, a 20-year-old developer from **Sargodha, Pakistan**, who thrives on turning complex problems into elegant, user-centric solutions. My passion lies in the full spectrum of development, from crafting responsive front-ends with **Next.js** to engineering robust back-end systems with **Node.js** and **Kotlin**.

I believe in writing clean, maintainable code and am always eager to explore new technologies that can elevate the projects I work on.

- 🌱 **Currently Exploring:** Advanced System Design, MLOps fundamentals, and diving deeper into Android development with Kotlin.
- 👯 **Looking to Collaborate:** On open-source projects that make a tangible difference.
- 💬 **Ask Me About:** MERN stack, RESTful API design, or how to structure a Next.js project for scale.
- ⚡ **Fun Fact:** My debugging skills are powered by copious amounts of *chai* (tea).
